Make It Yours!

Create custom watches for your company, your non-profit fundraiser, to put into a time capsule, or for any other reason.

Lots of people know us for our incredible good looks (and the watches that you can buy on this-here-website). We also have a dark secret: We love creating custom watches for companies, non-profits and personal events.

If you are interested in customizing a set of watches, whether simply engraving the back of the watches you see on the site or completely designing a watch for yourself, let us know by emailing Aaron at custom@modifywatches.com. Our minimum order is 50 Modify Watches for engraving and 100 for a full customization.
