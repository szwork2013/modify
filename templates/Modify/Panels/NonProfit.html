Non-Profit

We have two programs to give back. The first is the Together watch (you see, the design of the watch says “Together” on it). This is a limited-edition timepiece. Every month we rotate one core non-profit partner - $20 of each watch sale goes to that partner. To date our ModiFamily has raised over $5,000 for the following:

  • Carrier Classic
  • Larkin Street Youth Services
  • La Casa De Las Madres
  • Wonderroot
  • Realize the Dream Institute
  • BAYCAT
  • Action in Africa
  • Young Survival Coalition

We also love donating product for silent auctions. It’s a fun and unique gift that can help raise money for causes that are important to you.
