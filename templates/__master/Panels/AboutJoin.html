Campus Rep Supervisor Position

Modify Watches is officially launching its Campus Rep program this Fall semester / quarter and is seeking responsible, full-time students for its Campus Rep Supervisor position. Candidates for the Campus Supervisor position should meet following qualifications:

  • One full-time year of University classes completed
  • Experience in a meaningful leadership role
  • Proven ability to handle large amounts of responsibility while tending to numerous, simultaneous projects and assignments
  • Confidence and comfort leading peers and managing others (you may even manage Reps that are older than yourself)
  • A creative mind
  • A self-starter
  • A competitive spirit
The Modify Campus Rep Supervisor position is a commission-based paid-internship that will run year-round. Performance will be measured each semester / quarter to determine participants' effectiveness and contribution.

  Responsibilities:
A great deal of responsibility will lie on the Campus Rep Supervisors. Supervisors will be responsible for taking the initiative to become integrated with the campus community, market and sell the watches, as well as manage other Modify campus' representatives. Supervisors will be responsible for motivating the team to drive sales and will be compensated accordingly. Campus Rep Supervisors will serve as the point person between Campus Reps and the management team at Modify Watches.

Benefits:

  • Real-world sales, marketing, and managerial experience
  • The opportunity to earn money while still enrolled in school (the sky's the limit…the more watches you and your team sells -- the more money in the bank!)
  • The opportunity to join a start-up and work with two Haas MBAs (mentorship opportunities included)
  • Free watches to build out your own Mod set, so you'll carry some swag on your wrist daily
